Chocolate Chip Cookies

  1. Ingredients

    • 8 tablespoons of salted butter
    • 1/2 cup white sugar
    • 1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla
    • 1 egg
    • 1 1/2 all purpose flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 3/4 cup chocolate chips

  2. Prep

    • Wash your hands
    • Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
    • Get a large bowl and all your ingredients ready

  3. Mix Wet Ingredients

    1. Microwave your butter for 40 seconds, then let it cool for a few minutes
    2. Add 1/2 cup white sugar and 1/4 cup packed light brown sugar to the bowl. At a stand mixer, beat butter and sugars to get them nice and fluffy
    3. Add egg and vanilla into the mixer and beat for 10-15 seconds

  4. Mix in Dry Ingredients

    1. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt
    2. Mix together until crumbles form, then use your hands to press the crumbles together into a dough
    3. Add the chocolate chips and mix them in with your hands

  5. Bake the cookies

    1. Roll the dough into 12 large balls and place on a cookie sheet
    2. Bake for 9-11 minutes until the cookies look puffy, dry, and just barely golden
    3. Let them cool for about 30 minutes

  6. ENJOY

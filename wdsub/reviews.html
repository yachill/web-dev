Sunrise on the Reaping

The Hunger Games prequel, Sunrise on the Reaping, tells the backstory of the beloved Haymitch Abernathy, Katniss and Peeta's mentor. It is a dystopian novel and the second prequel to the original Hunger Games trilogy. It is a grief-filled, traumatic story that connects all the previous books together. Haymitch's story will fly by when reading, but will stick with you afterwards. Get ready for a good cry. If you enjoy the Hunger Games, Sunrise on the Reaping is a must-read.
